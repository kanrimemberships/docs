This is the very first step — creating your school and connecting Kanri to Stripe so you can collect payments.

## What this page will cover

- Signing up for Kanri for the first time
- Agreeing to terms of service, privacy policy, and service agreement
- The Stripe onboarding process step-by-step (what information Stripe will ask for)
- What happens after onboarding is complete
- Common Stripe onboarding errors and how to fix them

## Steps outline

1. Log in or create a Kanri account at kanrimemberships.com
2. Click "New School" or follow the prompt to create your first school
3. Review and agree to all three agreements
4. Click through to Stripe onboarding
5. Complete Stripe's form: personal details, business details, bank account
6. Return to Kanri — your school is ready
7. You'll land on the Home dashboard

## What you'll need for Stripe

- Legal name and date of birth
- Business name and address
- Business type (sole trader, company, etc.)
- Bank account number and routing/sort code for payouts
- Government-issued ID may be required depending on your country
