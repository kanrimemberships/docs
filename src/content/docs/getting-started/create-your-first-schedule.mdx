---
title: Create Your First Schedule
description: How to create a recurring class schedule in Kanri, add session times in the visual editor, activate it, and confirm sessions are generating.
---

A **Schedule** is a named timetable that holds one or more recurring class entries. For example, you might create a schedule called "Beginner Karate" and add entries for Monday 6–7 pm and Wednesday 6–7 pm. Once activated, Kanri automatically generates individual [**Sessions**](/schedules/) from those entries each day going forward. Sessions are what members check in to and what staff take attendance on.

## Before you start

You'll need at least one [program](/getting-started/create-your-first-program/) with at least one class before you can create a schedule.

## Step 1: Create the schedule

1. Go to **Schedules** in the main navigation.
2. Click **New Schedule**.
3. Enter a **Name** for the schedule (e.g. "Beginner Karate").
4. Click **Create Schedule**.

Kanri creates the schedule and opens the visual schedule editor.

## Step 2: Add your class times in the editor

The schedule editor shows a weekly grid with days across the top and hours down the side. You build your timetable by adding session blocks directly onto the grid.

1. **Double-click** an empty time slot to add a session at that point. Or **click and drag** to draw the session block directly, from the start time to the end time.
2. A panel opens on the side. Select the **Program** and **Class** for this session. Optionally select a **Class Variant** and enter a **Location** (e.g. "Main Hall").
3. Click **Save Changes** in the panel to confirm the session.
4. Repeat for each class time you want to add. You can add multiple sessions across different days and classes in the same schedule.
5. When you're done, click the **Save** button in the top right of the editor to save your changes.

:::caution
Make sure you click **Save** in the editor before moving on. If you leave without saving, your session entries will be lost.
:::

## Step 3: Activate the schedule

New schedules are inactive by default. Activating tells Kanri to start generating sessions.

1. Click **Activate** on the schedule page.
2. Confirm by clicking **Activate** again.

Kanri will now generate sessions for this schedule going forward. Sessions are not created retroactively.

## Step 4: Confirm your sessions

After activating, the schedule page shows the upcoming sessions Kanri has generated. If the times or dates look wrong, check your school's [timezone setting](/getting-started/initial-settings/). That's the most common cause of incorrect session times.

:::note
If you need to change the schedule after activating it, for example to add a day or update the time, edit the sessions in the editor and click **Save**. Then click [**Sync**](/schedules/sync-schedule/) on the schedule page to apply your changes to future sessions without affecting past ones.
:::

:::tip
After activating, you can click **View Current Schedule** on the Schedules page to see an overview of all currently active schedules combined into one view.
:::

## What's a session?

Each session is a single class occurrence with a date and time. When a member checks in at the kiosk or you take attendance, it's recorded against a specific session. Sessions are created automatically going forward based on your schedule.
