Once your school is created, take a few minutes to set up the essentials before adding members or schedules.

## What this page will cover

- Setting your timezone (critical — affects all class times and reports)
- Configuring tax settings (jurisdiction, rate, included vs on-top)
- Customising your membership status labels (Active, Inactive + custom)
- Setting default active/inactive statuses
- Customising your level name (e.g. "Belt", "Grade") if you use a ranking system
- Uploading your logo and setting your brand colour

## Settings to configure (in order)

1. **Timezone** — Settings → General
2. **Tax settings** — Settings → General (if you charge tax)
3. **Membership statuses** — Settings → Membership Statuses (rename defaults, add extras like "On Hold" or "Trial")
4. **Level name** — Settings → General (only if you use levels/ranks)
5. **Branding** — Settings → Branding (logo, brand colour)
