## What this page will cover

- School gallery vs member gallery: the gallery in this section is school-wide (visible to all members and staff); member galleries are private to that member and managers
- Supported file types: photos (JPG, PNG, WebP), videos (MP4), documents (PDF)
- Storage and file size considerations
- Who can see the school gallery (all logged-in members, depending on visibility settings)
- Who can manage the gallery (staff with gallery permissions)
