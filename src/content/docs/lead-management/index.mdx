Lead Management is how Kanri helps you turn interested people into paying members. It covers the full journey a prospective member takes: from discovering your school online, to submitting their details, to booking and attending a trial class, to signing a waiver, to becoming a full member.

## How leads work

When someone submits your [Lead Page](/lead-management/lead-page/) — the public sign-up form for your school — Kanri automatically creates a member profile for them with the type set to **Lead**. You can also manually create a member profile and set the type to Lead yourself.

A Lead profile works exactly like any other member profile. You can add notes, send messages, view attendance, and assign a membership. The Lead type is just a label that tells you this person hasn't committed yet.

Each Lead has a **lead status** that tracks where they are in your pipeline:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **New** | Just submitted the lead form or was added manually. No one has followed up yet. |
| **Contacted** | Someone from your school has reached out to them. |
| **Qualified** | The lead is interested and likely to join. |
| **Trial** | Automatically set when the lead is given a trial subscription. |
| **Converted** | Set when the lead converts to a full paid membership. |
| **Lost** | No longer interested — not going to join. |

To update a lead's status, open their member profile and click **Edit**. The Lead Status field is on the edit form.

## Converting a lead

A lead becomes a Member automatically when they are given an active [membership](/members/memberships/) or [subscription](/members/subscriptions/). At that point, Kanri updates their type from Lead to Member.

You don't need to do anything special to "convert" a lead. Just add their membership or subscription as you would for any new member.
