Lead Management is how Kanri helps you turn interested people into paying members. It covers the full journey a prospective member takes: from discovering your school, to submitting their details, to booking and attending a trial class, to signing a waiver, to becoming a full member.

At the centre of this is your **[Lead Page](/lead-management/lead-page/)**: a public URL anyone can visit without logging in. Prospective members fill in their name, contact details, and how they heard about you. From there, they can optionally book a trial class directly from the page and sign a digital waiver before their first visit.

Once someone submits the form, they appear in your **leads list** inside Kanri. From there you can:

- See each lead's details, trial booking, and waiver status
- Follow up by sending a message directly from the lead record
- Move leads through your pipeline as they progress
- Convert a lead into a full member profile with a single action when they're ready to join
