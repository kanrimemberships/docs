Lead Management is how Kanri helps you turn interested people into paying members. It covers two things: the **Lead Page**, a public form where prospective members submit their details, and the **leads list**, where you track and follow up with those enquiries.

When someone fills in your Lead Page, they become a lead in Kanri. You can see their details, follow up by message, and when they're ready to join, convert them into a full member profile with a single action.

## What's in this section

[**Lead Page**](/lead-management/lead-page/) covers your school's public-facing enrolment form — how to enable it, configure trial bookings and waivers, and share the link with prospective members.

[**View and Convert Leads**](/lead-management/view-leads/) covers the leads list inside Kanri — how to filter, follow up, and convert leads into members.
