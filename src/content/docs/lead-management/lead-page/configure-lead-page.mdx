## What this page will cover

- Enabling trial class bookings on the Lead Page (prospective members can pick a class and time)
- Setting the trial booking window (how many days in advance they can book)
- Enabling waiver signing on the Lead Page (prospective members sign before their first class)
- What the waiver contains and where it is configured (link to the waiver template settings)
- Combining these features: all three settings are independent toggles
