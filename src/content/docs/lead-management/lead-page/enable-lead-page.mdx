title: Enable the Lead Page

:::note[Professional & Enterprise]

:::

The Lead Page is off by default. Before prospective members can use it, you need to turn it on.

## Before you start

You'll need the **Manage Settings** permission to change Lead Page settings. If you don't see the Settings menu, ask your school owner to grant you access.

## Turning on the Lead Page

1. Go to **Settings** in the main navigation.
2. Click the **Leads** tab.
3. Toggle **Enable Lead Page** to on.
4. Click **Save**.

Once enabled, your Lead Page is live immediately. Anyone with the link can visit it and submit the form.

## Writing your welcome message

The **Lead Page Welcome Text** is a short message that prospective members see at the top of the form. Use it to introduce your school, set expectations, or tell them what to expect next.

To set or update the welcome message:

1. Go to **Settings → Leads**.
2. Type your message in the **Lead Page Welcome Text** box.
3. Click **Save**.

If you leave this blank, the form still works — it just shows your school name without any additional introduction.

:::tip
Keep the welcome message short and friendly. One or two sentences is usually enough. For example: "Welcome to Springfield Karate! Fill in your details below and we'll be in touch soon."
:::

## Disabling the Lead Page

To turn off the Lead Page, toggle **Enable Lead Page** off and click **Save**. When disabled, visiting the Lead Page URL shows a message that the page is not currently available. Existing lead profiles in Kanri are not affected.
