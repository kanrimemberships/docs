The Lead Page is a public URL for your school that anyone can visit without logging in. It is how prospective members discover and sign up for your school online. They can submit their contact details, optionally book a trial class from your live schedule, and optionally sign a digital waiver before their first visit.

## What this page will cover

- What the Lead Page is and how it fits into Lead Management
- The Lead Page URL format: your school's public entry point
- What fields a prospective member fills in (name, email, phone, referral source, optional waiver, optional trial booking)
- Optional features: trial class booking and digital waiver signing — both controlled separately in settings
- How submitted leads appear in Kanri's leads list
- The difference between a lead and a member
