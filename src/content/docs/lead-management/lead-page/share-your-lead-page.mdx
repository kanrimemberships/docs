## What this page will cover

- Where to find your Lead Page URL in Kanri
- Sharing the link: putting it on your website, social media bio, Facebook page, Google Business profile
- Using a vanity URL to make the link cleaner (e.g. yourschool.kanri.app instead of the default URL)
- Embedding the link in email signatures
- QR code for in-person sharing (e.g. on posters at the front desk)
