Leads are stored as member profiles in Kanri, with their type set to **Lead**. There is no separate leads pipeline screen. Instead, you use the [Members list](/members/) with the Member Type filter to view and work with your leads.

## Finding your leads

1. Go to **Members** in the main navigation.
2. Check **Show inactive** in the filter bar — leads do not have active memberships, so they are hidden by default.
3. Open the **Member Type** dropdown and select **Lead**.

The list now shows only your leads. You can combine this with the name, age, or phone filters to narrow down further.

## Lead statuses

Each lead has a status that shows where they are in your follow-up process:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **New** | Just added — no one has followed up yet. |
| **Contacted** | Someone from your school has reached out. |
| **Qualified** | The lead is interested and likely to join. |
| **Trial** | Automatically set when the lead is given a trial subscription. |
| **Converted** | Set when the lead signs up as a paying member. |
| **Lost** | No longer interested. |

The lead status is visible on the member's profile when the **Lead** section is shown. To update it, open the member's profile and click **Edit**.

## Following up with a lead

You can contact a lead directly from their member profile:

- Click the member's name to open their profile.
- Go to the **Messages** tab to send an email or SMS.
- Use the **Notes** tab to record follow-up notes for your team.
- Use the **Interactions** tab to log calls, incidents, or other events.

## Converting a lead to a member

A lead converts automatically when you give them an active [membership](/members/memberships/) or [subscription](/members/subscriptions/). Kanri updates their member type from **Lead** to **Member** at that point.

To convert a lead:

1. Open the lead's profile.
2. Go to the **Memberships** tab and [assign a membership](/members/memberships/assign-membership/).
3. If they are paying, go to the **Subscriptions** tab and [create a subscription](/members/subscriptions/create-subscription/).

Once an active membership exists, the profile type changes to **Member** and the lead disappears from the Lead filter.
