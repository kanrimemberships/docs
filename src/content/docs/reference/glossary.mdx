## What this page will cover

Alphabetical list of every Kanri term with a plain-English definition. Planned entries include:

- **Attendance** — a record that a member was present for a class session
- **Billing Plan** — a named product that members can be subscribed to (e.g. "Monthly Karate")
- **Booking** — a reservation for a specific session; can be confirmed or pending
- **Class** — a type of training within a program (e.g. "Beginner Class" within the "Adult Karate" program)
- **Class Variant** — a specific time slot for a class (e.g. Monday 6pm, Wednesday 7pm)
- **Contact** — a person linked to a member (parent, guardian, emergency contact, or billing contact)
- **Gallery** — a collection of photos, videos, or files for a school or individual member
- **Group** — a named collection of members used for targeting broadcasts
- **Interaction** — an immutable log entry on a member's record (incident, call, meeting note)
- **Kiosk** — the self-check-in screen used by members to sign themselves into classes
- **Lead** — a prospective member who submitted their details via the Lead Page
- **Lead Page** — the public-facing enrolment form for prospective members
- **Level** — a rank within a program (e.g. White Belt, Yellow Belt) with promotion requirements
- **Member** — a person enrolled at your school
- **Member Number** — a numeric shortcut ID for kiosk sign-in
- **Member Portal** — the restricted Kanri view available to members when they log in
- **Membership** — an enrolment record linking a member to a program (the "what are they enrolled in?" record)
- **Note** — a mutable record on a member's profile (medical conditions, discounts, preferences)
- **Price** — a specific amount and billing interval within a billing plan (e.g. $120/month Adult rate)
- **Program** — a category of training at your school (e.g. "Adult Karate", "Junior Ballet")
- **Promotion** — advancing a member from one level to the next
- **Schedule** — a recurring class timetable that generates individual sessions
- **Session** — a single occurrence of a scheduled class (e.g. Monday 6pm on 15 Jan)
- **Subscription** — a billing record that charges a member on a recurring basis (the "how are they paying?" record)
- **Sync** — the action of updating future sessions to match the current schedule template
