## Usage fees

Usage fees apply on top of your monthly plan. Every plan includes C$10/month in usage credits, which covers most schools' SMS costs.

### SMS

| Type | Cost per message |
|---|---|
| SMS (per segment) | C$0.04 |
| MMS (media message) | C$0.08 |

A single SMS message is one segment if it is 160 characters or fewer. Longer messages are split into multiple segments and billed per segment.

### Transaction fees

These fees apply to all payments processed through Kanri's billing system (powered by Stripe). They are separate from your Kanri plan price and are deducted from each transaction before your payout.

| Payment method | Fee |
|---|---|
| Credit card (card not present, e.g. recurring billing) | 4.0% + C$0.30 |
| Bank account (pre-authorized debit) | 2.5% + C$0.40 |
| Credit card (card present, e.g. kiosk or in-person) | 3.5% + C$0.05 |
| Interac Debit | 1.5% + C$0.15 |

## Changing your plan

You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from [Settings → Usage](/settings/usage/). Changes take effect at the start of your next billing period.

If you downgrade and your school is using features not available on the lower plan (such as Lead Management on a Starter plan), those features will become inaccessible but your existing data is not deleted.
