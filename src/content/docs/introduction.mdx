Kanri is a school management platform built for martial arts dojos, dance studios, gymnastics clubs, swim schools, and any other activity-based school. It handles your members, classes, schedules, billing, and communication — all in one place, with no technical experience needed.

Before diving in, we recommend reading [How It All Fits Together](/how-it-all-fits-together/). It explains how the key concepts in Kanri connect to each other, which makes every other page much easier to follow.

If you're setting up Kanri for the first time, start with the [Getting Started guide](/getting-started/). If you already know what you're looking for, use the search bar at the top of the sidebar or browse the sections below.

## What Kanri can do

**[Members](/members/)** — your student database. Add members, track their contact details, keep notes, view their attendance history, and manage their enrolments and billing from one place.

**[Programs](/programs/)** — the activities you offer, like "Adult Karate" or "Junior Ballet". Programs contain classes and an optional belt or level system. Members are enrolled into programs via [Memberships](/members/memberships/).

**[Schedules](/schedules/)** — your class timetable. Create recurring schedules, take attendance, and (on Professional and Enterprise plans) let members book into sessions in advance.

**[Billing](/billing/)** — create billing plans and prices, attach subscriptions to members, and collect payments automatically. Includes payouts, payment history, and help with failed or disputed payments.

**[Messages](/messages/)** — send email or SMS to one member or a whole group, from scratch or using templates.

**[Groups](/groups/)** — organise members into named collections to make broadcasting and targeting easier.

**[Reports](/reports/)** — understand what's happening at your school: attendance trends, revenue, who's ready for promotion, who hasn't been in lately, and more.

**[Gallery](/gallery/)** — upload photos, videos, and files for the whole school or for individual members.

**[Kiosk](/kiosk/)** — a self-check-in screen for your front desk. Members find their name or enter their number and sign themselves into class.

**[Lead Management](/lead-management/)** *(Professional & Enterprise)* — a public page where prospective members can submit their details, book a trial class, and sign a waiver. Leads are tracked and can be converted into full member profiles with one click.

**[Member Portal](/member-portal/)** — what members see when they log in themselves: their profile, upcoming bookings, membership, and payment history.

**[Audit Log](/logs/)** *(Professional & Enterprise)* — a permanent, tamper-proof record of every action taken at your school.

**[Settings](/settings/)** — configure your school's branding, staff roles, notification behaviors, message templates, and more.

## Plans

Kanri is available on three plans: **Starter**, **Professional**, and **Enterprise**. All plans include the core features — members, billing, scheduling, and the kiosk. Professional and Enterprise unlock features like Lead Management, the Audit Log, and member self-booking. See [Plans & Pricing](/reference/plans/) for the full comparison.
