---
title: Schedules & Bookings
description: A reference for how Kanri models recurring classes, individual sessions, and member bookings.
---

## What this page will cover

- The three-layer model: Schedule → Session → Booking
- What a Schedule is (a recurring template: day, time, class, capacity)
- What a Session is (a single occurrence of a schedule on a specific date)
- What a Booking is (a member's reservation for a specific session)
- How sessions are generated from schedules (automatically, one at a time going forward)
- Capacity: how it limits bookings and what happens when a session is full
- Booking window: how far in advance members can book (configured in Settings → Booking Settings)
- Trial bookings vs regular bookings (different booking window)
