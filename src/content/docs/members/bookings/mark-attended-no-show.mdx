## What this page will cover

- The difference between "booked" and "attended" — a booking doesn't automatically mean they came
- Marking a booking as attended (creates a confirmed attendance record)
- Marking a booking as no-show (booking stays, no attendance recorded)
- How this differs from taking bulk attendance from the schedule view
- Correcting a mark-attended if you made a mistake
