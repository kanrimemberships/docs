Change a membership status when a member's enrolment changes.

## Understand membership statuses

Membership statuses are defined by your school. Each status is marked as either **active** or **inactive**.

This means you can create status names that fit your process, such as:

- Active
- On Hold
- Trial
- Withdrawn

## Change a membership status

1. Open the member from the [Members list](/members/).
2. Click **Memberships**.
3. Open the membership you want to update.
4. Click **Edit**.
5. Choose the new **Status**.
6. Review any other membership fields.
7. Click **Save**.

:::note
Kanri uses the active or inactive setting on the status itself. The status name alone does not decide whether the membership is active.
:::

## When to change status

Change status when you need to reflect a real enrolment change, such as:

1. a student becoming active
2. a student going on hold
3. a student returning after a break
4. a student leaving the program
