End a membership when the member is no longer enrolled in that program.

In Kanri, there is no separate close or end button for memberships. You end a membership by changing it to an **inactive** status.

## End a membership

1. Open the member from the [Members list](/members/).
2. Click **Memberships**.
3. Open the membership you want to end.
4. Click **Edit**.
5. Choose an **inactive** status.
6. Review the membership details.
7. Click **Save**.

## Choose the right inactive status

Your school may use different inactive statuses for different situations, such as:

- Cancelled
- Withdrawn
- Graduated
- Finished Trial

:::note
Ending a membership does not automatically cancel the member's [subscription](/members/subscriptions/). Check billing separately if the member also needs payments to stop.
:::

:::caution
If the member should stop being charged, also [cancel the subscription](/members/subscriptions/cancel-subscription/). Memberships and subscriptions are separate records.
:::
