A [membership](/reference/glossary/) is the member's enrolment record.

It answers the question: **Which program is this member enrolled in?**

This is different from a [subscription](/members/subscriptions/), which answers: **How and when does this member pay?**

## Understand what a membership includes

A membership can store:

- the [program](/programs/) the member is enrolled in
- their current level, if your school uses [Levels](/programs/levels/)
- their membership status
- sessions remaining, if you track classes in blocks
- the last promotion date, if your school uses levels

## Know how statuses work

Membership statuses are set by your school. They are not fixed across all schools.

Each status has:

- a name, such as `Active`, `On Hold`, or `Withdrawn`
- an active or inactive setting, which controls how Kanri treats that membership

## Important rules

- A member can only have **one membership per program** at a time.
- Ending a membership means changing it to an **inactive** status.
- Memberships and subscriptions are independent. A member can have one without the other.

:::note
Use the [Only Memberships report](/reports/memberships/only-memberships/) and [Only Subscriptions report](/reports/memberships/only-subscriptions/) to find mismatches between enrolment and billing.
:::
