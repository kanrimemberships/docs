## What this page will cover

- What a membership is (an enrolment record — this member is in this program, at this level, with this status)
- The difference between a membership and a subscription (critical — enrolment vs billing)
- Membership status: what Active/Inactive/custom statuses mean
- The membership timeline: start date, end date, status history
- What a level on a membership means (current belt/grade)
- Membership history: past memberships are kept for reporting even after they end
- One member can have multiple memberships (in different programs simultaneously)
