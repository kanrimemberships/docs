Promote a member when they are ready for the next level in their program.

## Before you start

Make sure:

1. Your school uses [Levels](/programs/levels/).
2. The membership already has a current level.
3. The current level has a **next level** set in your level settings.

## Promote a membership to the next level

1. Open the member from the [Members list](/members/).
2. Click **Memberships**.
3. Open the membership.
4. Click **Promote**.
5. Review the current level and the next level shown on screen.
6. Enter a promotion date if you want to record one.
7. Click **Promote**.

Kanri advances the membership to the **next** level in that program's level sequence.

:::note
You cannot pick any level from the list on this page. Promotion always moves forward to the next level that has been configured in your [Levels](/programs/levels/) setup.
:::

:::tip
If a member is ready but you cannot promote them, check whether the current level has a next level linked in your level settings.
:::
