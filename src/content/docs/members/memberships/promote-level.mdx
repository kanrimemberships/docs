## What this page will cover

- What a promotion means (moving a member from their current level to the next one in the program)
- Checking if a member is eligible before promoting (min classes attended, min days since last promotion)
- How to promote from the member's membership page
- How to promote directly from the kiosk (if the kiosk behavior is enabled)
- What changes after a promotion (level on membership, last_promotion date resets, attendance counter resets)
- Using the Promotions report to find members who are ready
