title: Add a Contact
description: How to add a parent, guardian, or emergency contact to a member's profile.
---

## What this page will cover

- Adding a brand-new contact (name, email, phone, relationship)
- Linking an existing member as a contact (e.g. a parent who is also a member)
- Setting the relationship type
- What to do when one parent has multiple children at the school (add as contact on each child)
- Editing a contact's details after adding them
- Removing a contact from a member (does not delete the contact's own record)
