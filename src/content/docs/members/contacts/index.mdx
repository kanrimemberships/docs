## What this page will cover

- What a contact is (a person linked to a member — typically a parent, guardian, or emergency contact)
- When contacts are required vs optional (especially for junior members)
- The two types of contacts: linked members (another person already in Kanri) and standalone contacts (just name/email/phone)
- What a "billing contact" is and why it matters (the contact whose payment method is charged instead of the member's)
- Visibility rules: what contacts can see if they have a portal account
- The relationship types available (Parent, Guardian, Emergency Contact, Other)
