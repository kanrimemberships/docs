## What this page will cover

- What a billing contact is (the person whose card is charged for a member's subscription)
- Why billing contacts are used (parents paying for their child's classes)
- How to designate a contact as the billing contact
- How the billing contact adds their payment method (they receive a link to enter card details themselves)
- What happens if the billing contact is removed or changed
- Can a member have multiple billing contacts? (No — one at a time)
