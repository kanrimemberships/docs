## What this page will cover

- Prerequisites: member must have a payment method on file (or billing contact does)
- Selecting a billing plan and price from the member's Payments tab
- Setting the subscription start date
- What happens on the start date (first charge triggered)
- Understanding the billing interval and when subsequent charges occur
- Confirming the subscription is Active
- What to do if the first charge fails immediately
