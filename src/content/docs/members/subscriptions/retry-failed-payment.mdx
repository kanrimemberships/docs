## What this page will cover

- Why payments fail (expired card, insufficient funds, card blocked)
- Where failed payments appear (member's payment history, Failing Subscriptions report)
- How to contact the member to update their payment method
- How to retry a payment once the card is updated
- How many retries happen automatically (Stripe retry logic)
- What to do if the member disputes the charge
- When to cancel the subscription vs keep trying
