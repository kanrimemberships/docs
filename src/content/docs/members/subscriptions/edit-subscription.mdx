## What this page will cover

- What can be edited on an active subscription (plan, price, dates)
- What cannot be changed without cancelling and recreating (billing interval, currency)
- How to change a member from one price to another within the same plan
- How changing a price affects the next billing date
- Editing the subscription end date (for fixed-term subscriptions)
