## What this page will cover

- The difference between cancelling immediately vs cancelling at period end
- How to cancel from the member's subscription tab
- What happens to the member's membership status when a subscription is cancelled (depends on your Behaviors settings)
- Whether cancelled subscriptions can be reactivated (no — you create a new one)
- Archiving a cancelled subscription to keep the view tidy
- Handling refunds when cancelling (refunds are processed separately via Stripe)
