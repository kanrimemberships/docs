## What this page will cover

- What a subscription is (a billing record tying a member to a billing plan price, with automatic recurring charges)
- The full subscription lifecycle: Active → Cancelled → Archived
- The difference between a subscription and a membership (billing vs enrolment)
- Single subscription mode vs multiple subscription mode (school-level setting)
- How billing dates work (charge date, next charge date, proration)
- What happens when a subscription fails, is retried, or is cancelled
- The relationship between subscriptions and program access (if program access gating is enabled)
