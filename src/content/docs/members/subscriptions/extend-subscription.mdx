## What this page will cover

- What "extending" a subscription means (pushes the next billing date forward without charging)
- Common reasons to extend: school was closed, member was sick, goodwill gesture
- How to extend: number of days to push the next billing date by
- How extension affects the subscription timeline
- Whether extending creates a credit or just delays the next charge
