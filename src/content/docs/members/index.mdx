A **member** is a student or participant at your school. Every person you track in Kanri — whether they're actively training, on hold, or a past student — is a member.

## What this page will cover

- Every field on a member profile, explained in plain English
- The tabs on a member profile and what each one contains (Overview, Contacts, Memberships, Subscriptions, Attendance, Bookings, Messages, Interactions, Notes, Gallery, Logs)
- Member statuses and what they mean
- The difference between a member's login account and their profile record
- What "linking" a user to a member means (auto-link behavior)
- How member numbers work for kiosk check-in
- Privacy and data: what members can see about themselves in the portal

## Profile fields reference (outline)

- First name, last name
- Email address (used for messages and portal login)
- Phone number (used for SMS)
- Date of birth (used for birthday reports)
- Address
- Photo (used for kiosk identification)
- Member number (for kiosk numeric sign-in)
- Referral type (how they heard about the school)
- Member since date
