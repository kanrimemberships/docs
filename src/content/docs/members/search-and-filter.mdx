title: Search and Filter Members
description: How to find members using search, filters, and sorting in the members list.
---

The Members list has a set of filters that let you narrow down exactly who you're looking for. You can combine multiple filters at once.

## Finding a member

Go to **Members** in the main navigation. The filter bar is at the top of the list.

**Search by name:** Type in the **First Name** or **Last Name** fields. The search matches from the beginning of the name — for example, searching "che" will find "Chen" but not "Atcheson".

**Search by member number:** Enter the number in the **Number** field. Useful if you know the number but are unsure of the spelling of their name.

**Search by age:** Enter a number in the **Age** field to find members of a specific age.

## Showing inactive members

By default, the Members list shows only profiles that have at least one **active** [membership](/members/memberships/). Leads, contacts, and inactive members are hidden unless you ask to see them.

To see everyone, check the **Show inactive** checkbox in the filter bar.

:::tip
"Show inactive" is useful when looking for an old student who has stopped training, a parent profile (contacts), or anyone you know is in the system but can't find in the default view.
:::

## Filtering by member type

The Members list can contain students, leads, contacts, and staff. Use the **Member Type** dropdown to filter to a specific type:

| Type | Shows |
|---|---|
| **All** | Everyone in the database (subject to other filters) |
| **Member** | Active students with a membership or subscription |
| **Lead** | Prospective students not yet enrolled |
| **Contact** | Parents, guardians, and associated people |
| **Staff** | People with a staff role in Kanri |

See [Members overview](/members/) for a full explanation of member types.

## Additional filters (full access)

Staff with the **View Members (Full)** permission can also filter by:

- **Phone** — search by the start of a phone number (for example, entering "416" finds all profiles whose phone number starts with 416).
- **Quit since** — shows members who had an active membership but became inactive on or after the date you enter. Useful for identifying members who have recently quit.
- **Created since** — shows members who were first given an active membership on or after the date you enter. Useful for identifying new members who joined recently.

## Sorting the list

Click a column header to sort by that column. Click again to reverse the sort direction.

You can sort by:
- **First Name**
- **Last Name**
- **Last Attendance** — when the member last attended a confirmed class

## Exporting the list

To export the current filtered list as a CSV file, click the **Download** icon at the top of the table. The export includes the same data shown in the table, with the same filters applied.

## Why a member might not appear

If you're searching for someone and can't find them:

1. **They have no active membership.** Check the **Show inactive** box to include all profiles.
2. **The name is spelled differently.** Try searching just the first letter or two of their first or last name to cast a wider net.
3. **They were added under a different name.** Try searching by phone number instead. Note that this filter requires the **View Members (Full)** permission.
4. **Their profile was deleted.** Deleted profiles cannot be recovered from the Members list.
