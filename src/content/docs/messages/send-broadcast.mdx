title: Send a Broadcast
description: How to send an email or SMS message to multiple members at once.
---

## What this page will cover

- Going to Messages → Compose
- Choosing your recipient set:
  - All active members
  - Members in a specific program
  - Members in a specific group
  - Members with a specific membership status
- Choosing Email or SMS
- Writing the subject (email) and body
- Using a template to pre-fill the message
- Using placeholders (e.g. first name) for personalisation
- Previewing before sending
- Sending and what the confirmation looks like
- Warning: broadcasts are sent immediately to all recipients — double-check before hitting send
