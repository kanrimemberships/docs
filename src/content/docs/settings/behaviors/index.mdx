## What this page will cover

- What behaviors are: optional settings that change how Kanri works at your school
- Overview of behavior categories:
  - **General** — auto-link accounts to member profiles
  - **Kiosk** — what the kiosk allows and blocks
  - **Subscriptions** — single vs multiple subscriptions, billing plan modes, program access gating, receipts
- Why behaviors are separate from regular settings: they're powerful but can cause unexpected results if misconfigured
- Recommended behaviors for most schools
