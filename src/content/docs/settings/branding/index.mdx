## What this page will cover

- What branding in Kanri controls: logo and colour (used in emails and the kiosk), email sender address, SMS phone number, vanity URL
- Why branding matters: members see your school name and logo, not Kanri's
- Where branding appears: kiosk welcome screen, emails sent from Kanri, the Lead Page, member-facing receipts
