## What this page will cover

- What the kiosk is: a web-based self-check-in screen typically running on a tablet at reception or the training room entrance
- How check-in works: member finds their name or enters their member number, selects the class, checks in
- The live attendance feed: staff can see who is currently checked in
- Level promotion display: a congratulations screen can appear on promotion (configurable)
- How kiosk attendance feeds into the attendance history and program level progress
- Kiosk vs taking attendance as a staff member (both record the same way)
