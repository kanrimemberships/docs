## What this page will cover

- The kiosk is just a web browser — any tablet, laptop, or old screen with a browser works
- Opening the kiosk URL (found in your school settings)
- Putting the browser in full-screen mode (or kiosk mode on iPad/Android)
- Best practices: lock the device to just the kiosk URL, use a tablet stand or mount
- Recommended hardware (tablet recommendations for different budgets)
- Keeping the kiosk online (ensure stable Wi-Fi; the kiosk won't work offline)
