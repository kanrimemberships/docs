title: Why Can't a Member Check In?
description: Step-by-step guide to diagnosing why a member is blocked or can't sign in at the kiosk.
---

## What this page will cover

- Check 1: Does the member have an active membership? (Members without an active membership can't sign in to a class)
- Check 2: Is the "Block expired subscriptions" kiosk behavior enabled? If so, do they have an active subscription?
- Check 3: Is program access gating enabled? Does their subscription grant access to the program they're trying to sign in for?
- Check 4: Is the schedule showing correctly on the kiosk? (Might be deactivated, or outside the booking window)
- Check 5: Is the kiosk connected to the internet?
- How to allow a member in manually (take their attendance as staff instead)
