## What this page will cover

- Opening the Audit Log from the sidebar
- What each log entry shows: timestamp, actor (who did it), action type, subject (what was affected), detail
- Filtering by date range
- Filtering by action type (e.g. only show billing events, only show member changes)
- Searching by member name or staff member name
- Understanding the log format: what "system" actions are (automated events, not triggered by a specific user)
