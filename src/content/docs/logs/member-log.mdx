## What this page will cover

- Opening the log from a member's profile (the Log tab)
- What the member log shows (all actions related to that specific member: profile edits, membership changes, subscription events, attendance records, messages sent)
- How member logs differ from the school-wide log (filtered to just that member's activity)
- Using the member log to answer "what happened to this member?" questions
- Spotting errors: if something looks wrong in a member's record, the log shows you what changed and when
