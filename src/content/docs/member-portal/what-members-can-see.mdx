## What this page will cover

- The member's home/dashboard view
- Profile: viewing (but not fully editing) their own details
- Memberships: seeing which programs they're enrolled in and their current status
- Subscriptions: viewing their billing plan, subscription status, and next charge date
- Payment history: viewing past payments and receipts
- Upcoming bookings: seeing what classes they're booked into
- Gallery: accessing the school gallery and their personal gallery
- What members cannot change (billing details, membership status, etc.)
- How to get something changed (contact the school)
